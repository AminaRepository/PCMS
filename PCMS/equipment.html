Quality enjoyment Pool & Snooker tables

Since day one of our club we have offered the highest quality Pool tables in Old Town. We carefully consider each and every table we buy, we regularly mantain them and make sure they are modern, sleek and enjoyable to play on. Since 1993 we have also offered Snooker tables at our establishment, with a just as high quality control.

Quality equipment Pool & Snooker racks

Our establishment knows that just as important on the table you play on, is the rack you use. Our pool racks are regularly cleaned, polished and checked for any damage to ensure you have nothing to hinder your talent. Our snooker racks are looked after just as well, and with plenty of colours and options to choose from for either one you never have to worry about anything other than your next move.

Quality products Pool & Snooker cues

One of, if not the most important aspects of a good experience is a good cue. We have been carefully selecting our cues and make sure to offer our members only the best of the best. We even offer new cues to be sold to our members who want a more personal touch to their experience.
